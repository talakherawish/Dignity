# Dignity CMS — Coworker Guide

A quick guide for editors using the Dignity content management system (Payload CMS).

---

## Getting Access

Tala (content manager) will create your account and send you:
- The admin panel URL (e.g. `http://localhost:3000/admin` for local, or the deployed address)
- Your email address
- A temporary password — **change it on first login**

---

## Logging In

1. Open the admin panel URL in your browser
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **Login**

You'll land on the dashboard showing the collections you have access to.

---

## Your Role

There are two roles:

| Role | Can do |
|------|--------|
| **Editor** | Create and edit content in your assigned section. Cannot publish — save as Draft. |
| **Content Manager** | Full access: publish content, create/delete user accounts, manage all sections. |

As an **Editor**, your drafts go to the Content Manager for review and publishing. Content only appears on the website after it is published.

---

## Collections (What You Can Edit)

Depending on your assigned section, you'll see one or more of these:

| Collection | What it holds |
|---|---|
| **Articles** | News articles and press coverage |
| **Activities** | Seminars, conferences, meetings, Windsor-Birzeit events |
| **Media Updates** | Announcements, news items, photos, clippings |
| **Participants** | Fellows, faculty, researchers, students, visitors |

---

## Creating New Content

1. Click the collection name in the left sidebar (e.g. **Articles**)
2. Click **Create New** (top right)
3. Fill in the fields — titles, body text, dates, images, etc.
4. When done, click **Save Draft** at the bottom

> ⚠️ Do **not** click Publish — only the Content Manager publishes content.

---

## Editing Existing Content

1. Click the collection in the sidebar
2. Click on the item you want to edit
3. Make your changes
4. Click **Save Draft**

The Content Manager will review and publish your changes.

---

## Adding Images / Media

1. In the **Media** collection, click **Create New**
2. Click **Browse** and select your image file
3. Add an `Alt Text` description
4. Click **Save**

Then, when editing an Article or Activity, use the image picker field to attach your uploaded media.

---

## Tips

- All content is saved as **Draft** by default — nothing goes live until the Content Manager publishes it
- You can edit a draft as many times as needed before it's published
- If you make a mistake on published content, save a new draft and notify the Content Manager
- Arabic and English content go in separate fields — fill in both if you can
